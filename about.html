A letter from Susan:

Greetings,

My name is Susan Holland and I would like to take this opportunity to introduce myself as well as my new company, Holland's Investigative Services, LLC.

Holland's Investigative Services, LLC is a North Carolina State Licensed Detective Agency, License Number 221256. My office is located in Pink Hill, NC

I am a Retired Deputy with Duplin County Sheriff's Office. I retired as a Detective in the Criminal Investigations Unit on October 31, 2018. I have more than 13 years' experience in law enforcement. I attended Basic Law Enforcement Training at James Sprunt Community College in Kenansville, North Carolina in 2005. On August 20, 2005 I began my career at Duplin County Sheriff's Office as a Deputy in the Child Support Division. On April 19, 2010 I was transferred to the Narcotics Division where I was trained in surveillance techniques as well as other Investigative skills. On December 6, 2012 I was transferred to the Criminal Investigations Unit. During that time, I have been the lead investigator in several major felony investigations. I have taken numerous classes related to this position. I attended ICAC Training July 2015 in order to investigate crimes against children. I attended Coastal Plain Law Enforcement Training Center on January 16, 2016, Basic Interview and Interrogation, April 15, 2016, Basic Criminal Interrogation, April 29, 2016, and Advanced Interview and Interrogation. I attended the North Carolina Homicide investigators Association 2016 Fall Training Conference. In October of 2016, I attended the Legislative Update Training. I attended ICAC Investigative Techniques in February of 2017. On November 3, 2017, I attended the North Carolina Homicide Investigators Association 2017 Fall Training Conference.

After retirement I applied for a Private Investigator License and opened up Holland's Investigative Services, LLC to offer my services to those in Duplin and surrounding counties. My company will provide confidential and discreet investigations for those who enlist my services.

If you think that you could benefit from Holland's Investigative Services, LLC, I would be pleased to discuss any inquiries you may have. Please feel free to call me at 910-340-7000. I would welcome the opportunity to serve you in this capacity.
